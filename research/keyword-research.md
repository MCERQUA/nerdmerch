# Keyword Research - Nerdmerch

**Research Date:** March 23, 2026
**Target Audience:** Tech-savvy 18-40 year olds, gamers, internet culture enthusiasts
**Target Markets:** San Jose, Seattle, Austin, Boston, Denver, Portland, Minneapolis, Raleigh

---

## Executive Summary

The custom apparel printing space is competitive for generic terms ("custom t-shirt printing", "screen printing") but offers significant opportunities in long-tail, niche-specific, and location-based keywords. The highest-value opportunities combine service type with specific demographics (gamers, tech events) and geographic targeting. Nerdmerch should focus on low-competition, high-intent keywords that align with its target audience of tech-savvy individuals and gaming communities.

---

## Keyword Intent Classification

### Transactional Intent (Ready to Buy)
**High Priority:** Users searching with purchase intent
- "order custom t-shirts online"
- "custom t-shirt printing services"
- "bulk t-shirt printing near me"
- "same day custom t-shirt printing"
- "custom hoodie printing for teams"

### Informational Intent (Researching)
**Medium Priority:** Users looking for information, may convert later
- "how to choose t-shirt printing method"
- "DTG vs screen printing comparison"
- "custom t-shirt design tips"
- "what is the best printing method for small batches"
- "how much does custom t-shirt printing cost"

### Commercial Investigation (Comparing Options)
**Medium-High Priority:** Users comparing providers
- "best custom t-shirt printing companies"
- "printful vs custom ink comparison"
- "affordable custom t-shirt printing reviews"
- "local screen printing near me"
- "custom t-shirt printing for businesses"

### Navigational Intent (Looking for Specific Brand)
**Low Priority:** Users searching for specific companies
- "custom ink"
- "printful"
- "vistaprint custom shirts"
